2012

Jun 4th, 2012 - Domeo and Drupal 7 at SemTechBiz

Stephane Corlosquet presented some of the capabilities of the Drupal 7 platform at SemTechBiz (The Semantic Technology & Business Conference) in San Francisco. During the same presentation Dr. Ciccarese introduced Domeo and its export capabilities thorugh RDF and Annotation Ontology. Stephane and Paolo demonstrated the integration of a Drupal 7 application with Domeo through JSONP and JSON-LD.

Oct 17th, 2012 - Domeo and antibodyregistry.org at Neuroscience 2012

The integration of antibodyregistry.org and Domeo has been presented by Dr. Paolo Ciccarese in a poster session of the Neuroscience 2012 conference in New Orleans (LA, USA).

Testimonials

Anita de Waard,
Elsevier Labs, Researcher Disruptive Technologies

"Domeo is a unique and powerful toolkit, which combines automated semantic annotation and manual curation of scientific content, and allows these annotations to be shared with the community in a Linked Data environment.
